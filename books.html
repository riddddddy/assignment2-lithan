Our Newest Book Collection

Matilda

"Matilda" by Roald Dahl is a captivating tale about a brilliant young girl named Matilda Wormwood who discovers she has telekinetic powers. Neglected by her shallow parents, Matilda finds solace in books and forms a special bond with her kind teacher, Miss Honey. Together, they navigate the challenges posed by the tyrannical headmistress, Miss Trunchbull, and Matilda learns to harness her unique abilities to stand up against injustice and make a positive impact on those around her.

Four-Four-Two

"Four Four Two" is a novel by Dean Hughes that follows the lives of two brothers, Spencer and Dalton, during World War II. They are passionate about soccer and end up serving in different roles in the military—one as a soldier and the other as a medic. The book explores themes of brotherhood, sacrifice, and the impact of war on individuals and their families. Through their experiences, the brothers learn about courage, resilience, and the power of camaraderie both on and off the soccer field.

The life of a boy who dreams

"The Life of a Boy Who Dreams" is a poignant coming-of-age story that follows the journey of a young boy named Lucas, whose vivid imagination and ambitious dreams shape his worldview. From a small town where possibilities seem limited, Lucas envisions a future filled with adventure, success, and making a positive impact on the world around him. Through trials and triumphs, he navigates challenges, discovers his passions, and learns valuable life lessons about perseverance, resilience, and the importance of staying true to oneself amidst societal expectations. As Lucas grows, his dreams evolve, ultimately guiding him towards self-discovery and realizing his full potential.

Living in Russia tips and tricks

"Living in Russia" is an insightful guidebook that offers practical tips and cultural insights for navigating daily life in this vast and diverse country. From understanding Russian social etiquette and embracing the rich culinary traditions to navigating public transport and healthcare systems, this book equips readers with essential knowledge to thrive and enjoy their experience in Russia. Ideal for expatriates, travelers, and anyone curious about Russian culture, this compact guide provides a sneak peek into the nuances of daily life and cultural practices that make living in Russia a unique and enriching experience.
